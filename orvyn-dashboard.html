The Problem
Paralysis of Intent
After a stroke, patients retain the desire to move their hand — but the neural pathway to complete the movement is damaged. Their muscles receive a partial signal but cannot follow through, degrading quality of life.
The Solution
ORVYN Completes the Movement
ORVYN's EMG sensor detects the residual electrical signal in the forearm. Its proportional control system translates that intent — however weak — into full assisted finger movement via 4 servo motors and Kevlar tendons.
